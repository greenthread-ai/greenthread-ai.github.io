Hybrid Training

One GPU fleet for inference and training. GreenThread intelligently schedules training and fine-tuning jobs alongside production inference, maximizing utilization of your most valuable resources.

End the inference vs. training divide

Traditional infrastructure forces a choice: dedicate GPUs to inference or to training. Neither is fully utilized.

Inference workloads spike during business hours and quiet overnight. Training jobs need large GPU allocations but often sit in queues waiting for resources. Organizations with limited GPU availability—universities, research labs, enterprises—feel this pain most acutely. GreenThread eliminates this artificial divide.

Intelligent workload scheduling

Training jobs run when and where it makes sense—automatically.

GreenThread's scheduler analyzes multiple factors to find the optimal time to run training jobs. It shifts inference workloads across the fleet, consolidating them to free up contiguous GPU capacity for training. When inference demand returns, training checkpoints and gracefully yields resources.

Seamless workload transitions

Inference never stops. Training runs when there's room.

When a training job is scheduled, GreenThread orchestrates a graceful transition. Inference models are migrated to other nodes in the fleet using our Model Multiplexing technology. Once GPUs are freed, training begins. If inference demand spikes, training checkpoints its progress and yields—resuming automatically when capacity returns.

  • Zero inference downtime during training transitions
  • Automatic checkpointing preserves training progress
  • Priority controls ensure critical workloads always run
  • Works with any training framework (PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX)
